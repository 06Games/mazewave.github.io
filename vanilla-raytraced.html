PBR Resources for Minecraft

Vanilla Raytraced provides reflections, roughness, emissive light to your world with the same original look of Minecraft, but in better.

This resource pack only works on NVIDIA RTX cards.

This version of the pack is still in early developpement. You can deal with bugs while playing the game.
